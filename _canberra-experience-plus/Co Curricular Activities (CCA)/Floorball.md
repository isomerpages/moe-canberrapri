<p>The Floorball CCA provides students with opportunities to develop their
physical fitness, teamwork, and sportsmanship through structured training
and gameplay. Students learn fundamental floorball skills such as stick
handling, passing, receiving, and shooting, while gaining an understanding
of game rules and basic positioning. Through regular drills and small-sided
games, they develop coordination, agility, and ball control while learning
to work effectively with their teammates.</p>
<p>As students progress, they deepen their understanding of strategies and
movement on the court. They learn to communicate clearly, make quick decisions
during play, and support one another in achieving common goals. The CCA
also emphasises important values such as resilience, discipline, and respect,
while providing a positive environment for students to stay active and
build confidence.</p>
