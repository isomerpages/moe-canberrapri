<p>Aesthetics and English Week is an annual event that celebrates creativity
while nurturing values that truly matter. This year's focus on empathy
and care — for others, the environment, and ourselves — provided a meaningful
opportunity for the school community to pause, reflect, and reconnect through
the Arts and the English Language.</p>
<p>Throughout the week, students engaged in a range of fun and meaningful
activities. Storytelling sessions, puppet shows, and arts experiences encouraged
them to consider diverse perspectives and reflect on how their words and
actions impact others.</p>
<p>A key highlight was the Creative Arts Competition, which showcased thoughtful
and heartfelt stories and artworks centred on care and empathy. These entries
demonstrated not only creativity but also a growing awareness of the importance
of making a positive difference.</p>
<p>The week concluded on a joyful note with Storybook Character Day, where
students shared how their chosen characters inspire kindness and empathy.
Aesthetics and English Week serves as a reminder of the compassionate individuals
we hope our students will become. We are grateful for the strong support
of our parents, whose presence added warmth and reinforced these shared
values.</p>
