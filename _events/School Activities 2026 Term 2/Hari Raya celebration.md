<p><strong><u>2026 Hari Raya Celebrations</u></strong>
</p>
<p>Canberra Primary School celebrated Hari Raya 2026 with a vibrant and meaningful
event centred on the theme of the Arts. The concert brought together tradition
and innovation, creating a rich cultural experience for both performers
and audience.
<br>
<br>A key feature of the celebration was the live percussion segment, where
rhythmic performances of festive songs energized the atmosphere and showcased
students’ musical talents. This was complemented by a variety of student
performances, including silat show, singing and dance.
<br>
<br>Adding a unique and forward-looking dimension to the concert was the introduction
of an AI character as the fourth host. This creative integration of technology
not only engaged the audience but also demonstrated how digital tools can
enhance language learning and performance.</p>
<p>The event was a joyful celebration of culture, creativity and community.
It was heartening to witness our students take pride in their performances,
while the audience responded with enthusiasm and appreciation. The concert
truly captured the spirit of Hari Raya, togetherness, gratitude and joy
that leaves lasting memories for all who were present.</p>
