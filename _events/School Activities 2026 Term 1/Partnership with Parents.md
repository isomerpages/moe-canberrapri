<p><strong>Partnership with Parents</strong>
</p>
<p><strong>Parents Virtual Forum and Parents F2F Forum</strong>
</p>
<p>Level Parent Forums are a cornerstone of our commitment to building strong
partnerships between home and school in support of students’ holistic development.
These engagement sessions bring together parents from each level (P1 to
P6) to foster collaborative dialogue that enhances every child’s learning
journey.</p>
<p>The forums serve as important communication platforms, keeping parents
informed of key school developments while providing space for meaningful
exchange. During these sessions, parents receive guidance on supporting
children’s well-being, an overview of student management approaches, and
insights into our pedagogical practices across subjects. School leaders
also share level-specific information aligned to each year group’s developmental
needs and academic milestones, ensuring support is timely and relevant.</p>
<p>Parents are further equipped with practical strategies to extend learning
at home, helping to create a seamless and supportive learning environment
between school and family.</p>
<p>In recognition of different developmental needs, P1 and P6 forums are
conducted face-to-face to support key transition points. Forums for P2
to P5 are held virtually, offering flexibility while maintaining meaningful
engagement.</p>
<p>Beyond information sharing, the forums provide a platform for parents
to ask questions and share concerns. This two-way communication strengthens
trust, ensures responsiveness to community needs, and reinforces a strong
home–school partnership.</p>
