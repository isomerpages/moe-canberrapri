<p><strong><u>Primary 5 Outdoor Learning Experience Camp</u></strong>
</p>
<p>The Primary 5 Camp Programme was a meaningful and engaging learning experience
that extended students’ growth beyond the classroom. Through a carefully
designed sequence of outdoor activities and team challenges, students developed
resilience, adaptability and confidence as they stepped out of their comfort
zones. The camp provided authentic opportunities for experiential learning,
allowing students to discover their strengths and areas for improvement
in a supportive environment.</p>
<p>Strong partnerships between students, teachers and camp trainers were
central to the programme’s success. Teachers worked closely with trainers
to ensure activities were purposeful, safe and aligned to desired learning
outcomes. Positive interactions and consistent guidance fostered trust,
open communication and mutual respect. Students were encouraged to voice
their thoughts, support one another and reflect on their experiences.</p>
<p>Importantly, students demonstrated the ability to apply their learning
in real contexts. They practised teamwork during group initiatives, exercised
leadership in task delegation, and showed perseverance during physically
and mentally demanding activities. Many displayed improved problem-solving
skills and greater self-awareness by the end of the camp. Overall, the
programme strengthened character, collaboration and social-emotional competencies,
contributing meaningfully to their holistic development.</p>
