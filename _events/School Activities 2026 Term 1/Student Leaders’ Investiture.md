<p><strong><u>Student Leaders’ Investiture</u></strong>
</p>
<p>The Student Leaders' Investiture marks a significant milestone in recognising
students who have stepped forward to serve and lead within the school community.
This meaningful event affirms their roles and responsibilities while setting
clear expectations anchored in our school's iCREST values.</p>
<p>Through the investiture, students gain a deeper understanding that leadership
is not defined by titles but by the values they demonstrate through their
actions. Guided by the belief that Every Canberrian is a Leader, the ceremony
inspires students to lead with purpose, take initiative, and contribute
positively to the school environment.</p>
<p>Beyond recognising appointed student leaders, the investiture serves as
a powerful platform to inspire the wider student body. It encourages all
students to step forward, take initiative, and embody positive values in
their daily actions, reinforcing that everyone can be a role model and
make a meaningful difference.</p>
<p>Student leaders are provided with authentic opportunities to develop confidence,
communication, and teamwork skills as they support school programmes and
serve as role models to their peers. This event reflects our commitment
to nurturing values-driven leaders throughout the school.</p>
