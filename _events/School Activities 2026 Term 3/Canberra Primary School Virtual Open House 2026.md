<p>Canberra Primary School Virtual Open House 2026</p>
<p>At Canberra Primary School, every child is nurtured to become a Discoverer,
Learner, and Leader - curious, creative, confident, and ready to make a
positive difference.</p>
<p>Across every level, our students engage in meaningful learning experiences
that help them grow in character, confidence, and compassion. Our Primary
1 and 2 students embraced the theme "Together We Shine", learning the value
of supporting one another in class and at recess.</p>
<p>Through our Theatre Magic experience, our Primary 3 students explored
Malay, Chinese, and Indian folktales, celebrating Singapore's rich cultural
diversity while developing creativity and confidence. Our Primary 4 students
showcased their talents at Faculty 2’s inaugural Celebration of Learning
after exploring traditional musical instruments from different cultures.</p>
<p>Through Values-in-Action, our Primary 5 students cared for the community
by helping to keep public spaces clean. Our Primary 6 students visited
the Enabling Village, deepening their understanding of inclusion, empathy,
respect, and teamwork.</p>
<p>Read the full story at: <a href="https://www.canberrapri.moe.edu.sg/our-story-begins/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.canberrapri.moe.edu.sg/our-story-begins/</a>
</p>
<p>We hope you enjoy exploring our story and celebrating the experiences
that shape every Canberrian.</p>
