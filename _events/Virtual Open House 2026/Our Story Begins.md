<p>Dear Parents and Guardians,</p>
<p>Welcome to our Virtual Open House at Canberra Primary School!</p>
<p>Every great story begins with a single step, and we are delighted to have
you here at the very start of yours. Consider this your prelude - a warm
invitation to turn the pages and journey through the heart of Canberra
Primary School, where every child is nurtured to become a Discoverer, a
Learner, and a Leader.</p>
<p>Through the chapters ahead, you will catch a glimpse of who we are, hear
our voices, witness curiosity in action, and see how passions are kindled
and talents are grown. Each chapter tells a part of our story, and we hope
that by the end of it, you will see a place where your child truly belongs.</p>
<p>Welcome to the Canberra family. Let the story begin!</p>

<p><strong>Chapter 2: Every Canberrian a Discoverer, Learner and Leader</strong>
<br>
<br>This vision is at the heart of everything we do! We want every student
to be a&nbsp;<strong>Discoverer</strong> - curious, creative, and ready
to explore the world around them. As&nbsp;<strong>Learners</strong>, they
dive deep, think big, and fall in love with learning across all subjects.
We also help students grow as&nbsp;<strong>Leaders</strong>, encouraging
them to step up with confidence, kindness, and integrity - in class and
beyond.</p>
<p>&nbsp;With this mindset, every Canberrian is set to thrive and lead with
purpose. We’re shaping confident, capable individuals who are ready to
make a difference in the world!</p>

<p><strong>Chapter 3: Together We Shine</strong>
</p>
<p>Our theme “Together We Shine” reflects the spirit of friendship, care,
and teamwork that can be seen in the daily life of our school. Our Primary
One and Two students show the beauty of community and collaboration through
the simple yet meaningful ways in which they support one another in class,
during group work, and even during recess. Whether it is helping a friend,
encouraging a classmate, working together to complete tasks, or celebrating
each other’s achievements, our students remind us that learning and growing
are always better when we do it together.</p>
<p>Join us as we share the special moments that show how, in Faculty 1, we
truly shine brighter together.</p>
<p></p>

<p><strong>Chapter 4: Every Child a Star</strong> 
</p>
<p>Faculty 2’s theme, “Every Child a STAR”, celebrated the unique talents
and strengths of every student. Through meaningful learning experiences,
our students built confidence, expressed themselves, and discovered their
potential.</p>
<p>Our Primary 3 students explored Malay, Chinese, and Indian folktales through
theatre activities, developing their communication skills, creativity,
and confidence while appreciating stories from different cultures.</p>
<p>Our Primary 4 students took part in the Faculty’s first Celebration of
Learning, where they explored traditional musical instruments and showcased
their learning through performances. As part of the Curriculum Integration
programme, they also participated in learning journeys that helped them
understand the importance of sustainability through authentic, hands-on
experiences.</p>
<p></p>

<p><strong>Chapter 5: Together, We Achieve More</strong>
</p>
<p>Faculty 3’s theme, <em>“Together, We Achieve More”</em>, highlighted the
importance of teamwork. As part of this journey, our Primary 6 students
visited the Enabling Village, where they learnt about inclusion and empathy
through real-life experiences. Through this meaningful learning journey,
they gained a better understanding of how people of different abilities
live, learn, and work together in an inclusive community. The experience
helped them appreciate the value of respect, understanding, and working
together to achieve more.</p>
